Welcome to bri_cs_breakers instruction manual.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
It is based on the classic arkanoid game with a slight twist.Now it's not about clearing levels,it's about giving your best and get the highest score.
So compete to gain the highest score.

Instructions:-Use left and right arrow key to move the paddle across screen.Prevent the ball to fall off and get the highest score.

Powerups/Dropups:-In the game at certain point you may get an opportunity to catch a dropup which may either reduce the paddle size or increase the ball size.

Scoring:-You will gain one point for each brick destroyed and 5 points for each dropup picked irrespective of it's type.
